About Texas

Texas Int'l College, which has been running excellently under Texas Int'l Education Network (2010), is a dynamic educational institution with academic programs (+2 Level) in Science, Management, Law and Humanities with outstanding Board Results of more than 94%. Texas has been founded with a set of academicians and entrepreneurs to meet the rising demand for qualified and skilled manpower in the field of Science and Technology, Management along with Hotel Management and Humanities. Since its very inception, Texas remains as an invitation to learning by both theory and practice.

BSC.CSIT

Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (B.Sc. CSIT) is a four year course affiliated to Tribhuvan University designed to provide the students with all sorts of knowledge in the field of Information Technology and Computing.

BCA

Texas has launched BCA affiliated to Tribhuvan University. It is the 4 years program that runs on semester-system under the faculties of Humanities and Social Sciences.

BBM

Bachelor of Business Management (BBM) is a course of 4 years, 8 semester ofTribhuvan University, faculty of management. Each semester constitute of 16 weeks of intensive study. The program covers 120 credit hours.
